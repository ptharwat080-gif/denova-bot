Privacy Policy

Denova Dental Clinic — Last updated: July 19, 2026

Denova Dental Clinic ("the Clinic", "we") respects the privacy of people who contact us through WhatsApp, Messenger, and Instagram. This policy explains what information we collect through these channels, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information We Collect

  • Your name and phone/WhatsApp number.
  • The content of messages you send us via WhatsApp, Messenger, or Instagram.
  • Appointment and service inquiry details you share with us.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and manage appointment bookings.
  • To keep an internal booking record used only by clinic staff.
  • To provide automated replies to common questions using an AI assistant. Only the text of your message is used to generate a reply; it is not used to train any AI models.

Who We Share Data With

We do not sell or rent your data. Your messages are shared only with the service providers necessary to run this system: Meta (WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram), our AI provider (to generate replies), and Google (to store booking records internally).

Data Retention

We keep contact and booking data for as long as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data held by us at any time.
